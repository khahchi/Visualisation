Hate Crimes Incidents in the US (2010-2019)

This visualisation aims to demonstrate the trend of the hate crimes incidents based on different bias motivation in the US, especially in the New York state between the period from 2010 to 2019.

Summary of Incidents across the US

There are almost incidents every where across the country. Overall, the difference between states is not significant; however, we observe that some states have a much larger number of incidents compred to the rest, which are California, Washington, Michigan, Texas and New York.

Number of incidents in New York State

We first would like to look into the relationship between the population and the number of incidents. The data shown on the chart is the total number of incidents calculated from 2010 to 2019. As we can see on the graph, the total incidents are around 20 for the majority of counties, only 5 counties have a much bigger number of data. It can be explained by the fact that these counties have more citizens compared to the rest.
